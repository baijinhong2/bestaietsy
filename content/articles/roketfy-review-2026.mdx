## How to use Roketfy (3-step setup)

**Step 1: Sign up for the 7-day free trial.** Go to [roketfy.com](https://roketfy.com/?aff=bestaietsy) and create an account. No credit card required for the free plan. The 7-day Pro trial unlocks every feature so you can stress-test before paying.

**Step 2: Bind your Etsy shop.** Connect your live Etsy shop URL. Listing Checker will pull all active listings into the dashboard within a few minutes. This step is what unlocks bulk compliance scanning — without it, you are limited to single-listing checks.

**Step 3: Run the 5-dimension workflow.** Pick one listing and run it through Listing Checker first, then Keyword Research, then AI Writer, then Competitor Analysis. Treat Roketfy as a 5-step assembly line for each listing, not a one-click tool. This workflow alone is what saves the 6 hours/week we measured.

## 4 real ways to use Roketfy in your Etsy workflow

**Use 1: Write listing titles and descriptions with AI Writer.** Paste a product name, pick a target keyword, and Roketfy generates 3 title variants plus a 150-word description. In our test, 4 of 5 titles passed the 50-character Etsy limit on the first try. The descriptions needed 5-10 minutes of human editing to remove the ~15% repetition, but the SEO structure was already in place.

**Use 2: Find high-volume, low-competition keywords.** Run your core niche keyword through Keyword Research and filter by competition under 0.3. In 20 queries, we surfaced 5 long-tail keywords with 1,000+ monthly searches and competition below 0.3 — the sweet spot for new listings trying to rank. This is comparable to [eRank's keyword explorer](https://erank.com/), but with a slightly more modern UI.

**Use 3: Scan for compliance with Listing Checker.** This is Roketfy's strongest feature. On 7 listings, Listing Checker flagged 4 for missing material disclosures and age warnings under the 6/2 children policy. One was a false positive (a vintage adult product incorrectly tagged as a children product), but the other 3 were real and would have triggered Etsy takedown emails. The same scan also caught listings that contained terms conflicting with the 8/11/2026 fur ban — useful for vintage sellers in the [30-day countdown](https://www.etsy.com/seller-handbook).

**Use 4: Generate POD designs with Roketfy POD AI.** Input a style, a product type, and a color palette. Roketfy generates 4 design variants in about 30 seconds. Quality is usable for simple typography and pattern designs but narrower in artistic range than Midjourney. We treated it as a fast-iteration sketch tool, not a final design tool.

## Who Roketfy is for (and who should skip it)

**Audience 1: New Etsy sellers (0-100 listings).** Start on the free plan to test the workflow. If you find Listing Checker and AI Writer save you 3+ hours per week, upgrade to Starter at $5.99/mo for the 50-listing cap. The free plan is genuinely usable, not a crippled demo.

**Audience 2: Mid-size Etsy sellers (100-500 listings).** This is Roketfy's sweet spot. The Pro plan at $24.99/mo covers up to 200 listings and unlocks the full AI Writer, Keyword Research, and bulk Listing Checker. **Audience 3: Senior Etsy sellers (500+ listings).** You will hit the 200-listing cap on Pro and need the Expert plan at $49.99/mo, or consider pairing Roketfy with a dedicated SEO tool like [eRank](https://erank.com/) for deeper keyword data. Roketfy's Competitor Analysis module is also lighter than what 500+ listing sellers typically need.

## Roketfy vs eRank: which one should you pay for?

If you have to pick one, it depends on what you actually need:

- **Pick eRank** if you want the deepest keyword data, the best rank-tracking, and a free tier that covers basic SEO. Best for sellers who already write their own copy and just need SEO data.

## The honest disadvantages of Roketfy

We would not write a real review without these:

1. **Pro at $24.99/mo is 2.5x the price of eRank Pro at $9.99/mo.** If you only need keyword research, eRank is a better value.
2. **Listing Checker false-positives.** We saw 1 false flag in 7 listings (a vintage adult product flagged for children-policy issues). Manual review is still required.

4. **POD AI design variety is narrower than Midjourney.** Useful for typography and simple patterns, less so for high-craft artistic designs.
5. **Bulk keyword queries sometimes lag 5-10 seconds.** Annoying but not a dealbreaker.
6. **Chinese, Japanese, and Korean language support is not production-ready.** Fine for English-heavy Etsy shops, weak for Asia-market sellers.
