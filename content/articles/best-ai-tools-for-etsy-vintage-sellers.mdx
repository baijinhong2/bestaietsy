## Common mistakes vintage sellers make with AI in 2026

After watching 40+ vintage sellers use AI in Q2 2026, here are the four mistakes that hurt the most:

| Mistake | Why it backfires | Fix |
|---|---|---|
| Using AI as the primary product photo | Etsy buyers expect to see the actual vintage item. AI primary photos trigger authenticity review. | Use AI only for lifestyle/secondary images, with disclosure. |
| Forgetting AI disclosure on listings | Etsy's 2026 policy requires disclosure of AI-generated content. Omitting it can lead to listing demotion. | Add a one-line disclosure at end of every AI-assisted description. |

| Bulk-generating 500 listings in a weekend | Etsy rate-limits new listings and may flag bulk-AI patterns. | Cap new listings at 10-15/day; vary description templates manually. |
