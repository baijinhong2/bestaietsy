Etsy 8/11 Fur Policy: What's Allowed and What's Banned

✅ Allowed (still compliant) ❌ Banned (effective 8/11)
Leather (cow, pig, sheep, suede) Raw animal fur (mink, fox, rabbit, etc.)
Wool (all sheep varieties) Fur garments (coats, jackets, outerwear)
Sheepskin (coats, slippers, rugs) Fur accessories (hats, scarves, bags)
Mohair (from angora goats) Vintage fur (no longer exempt)
Animal specimens (taxidermy, Etsy pre-approval) Decorative fur (collars, trim, linings)
Faux fur (synthetic, plant-based) Mixed products where fur dominates
Key change: Etsy previously allowed 20+ year old fur under its vintage category. Effective August 11, 2026, even 100-year-old mink coats and antique fur stoles are banned. This is the central change affecting vintage sellers.
